## WHY AI

Three years ago, I went all-in on AI.

Tool-agnostic. Bought everything. Tested everything. Broke things. Rebuilt things. Watched the market move from novelty prompts to real operational leverage.

What I found is simple:

Small business owners now have access to enterprise-grade capability, but most are still using it like a search box.
They have the access.  They do not yet have the leverage.

> [!NOTE]
> The tools are no longer the constraint.  The constraint is **personalization**: configuring AI around the person, the files, the workflows, the voice, and the way the business actually runs.
> SAAS for one person businesses has no customisation built in. What we need is a reliable **personal operating systems**: tools configured around how you work, not generic software you are forced to adapt to.

## CLAUDE COWORK

Claude is my current tool of choice because its configuration model is the strongest I have used for turning AI into something operational.

Claude Cowork is what I set up for the people I work with.

Not a chatbot you copy-paste from.  More like a junior operator that works with your files, your apps, your tone, your templates, and your way of thinking. It can draft the invoice, format the report, prepare the weekly brief, structure the client follow-up, or turn messy notes into usable next actions.

Then it stops for your sign-off before anything irreversible happens.

> [!NOTE]
> The goal is not to replace the business owner.  
> The goal is to give them the kind of operational support usually reserved for people with a team.
